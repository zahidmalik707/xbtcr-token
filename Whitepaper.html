📄 XBTCR Whitepaper

🔹 Introduction

XBTCR is a decentralized BEP-20 token built on the Binance Smart Chain, created to provide a fair, transparent, and tax-free environment for traders. With a fully community-driven approach and a transparent supply mechanism, XBTCR aims to build long-term trust in the decentralized space.

🔹 Supply Details

  • Initial Supply: 100 Trillion
  • Burned: 99 Trillion (Transaction Hash: 0x46c1...a9f3)
  • Final Circulating Supply: 1 Trillion
  • Decimals: 18

🔹 Tax Structure

  • 2.00% — Allocated for marketing to ensure project visibility and partnerships.
  • 1.50% — Auto-burned from each transaction to create a deflationary mechanism.
  • Total Tax: 3.5% (applies on standard swaps, not basic transfers).

XBTCR Whitepaper

1. Introduction

XBTCR is a decentralized token launched on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) designed to offer transparent, tax-efficient, and community-centric trading without hidden barriers or unfair mechanics.

2. Vision & Mission

Our vision is to create a token ecosystem that promotes fairness, trust, and long-term value. The mission is to empower users through decentralization and transparency.

3. Problem & Solution

Many new tokens fail due to high tax, hidden fees, or unfair practices. XBTCR eliminates these problems with a clean token model — zero tax on transfers, no blacklist or whitelist issues, and full visibility of tokenomics and supply.

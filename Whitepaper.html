XBTCR Whitepaper

Overview: XBTCR is a Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20) token designed for fairness, transparency, and community ownership. Built without complex tax mechanics or blacklist systems, XBTCR ensures smooth trading and open access for all users.

Supply: The token had an initial supply of 100 Trillion. On July 8, 2025, 99 Trillion tokens were permanently burned in a public transaction (hash: 0x46c1...3b3a9f3), bringing the final supply down to 1 Trillion XBTCR.

Tax Structure: A total of 3.5% tax is applied on each transaction: 2.00% goes to Marketing and 1.50% is automatically burned from every trade to reduce circulating supply.

Slippage: The recommended slippage is 2% for all buy/sell transactions to ensure smooth execution, especially during high activity periods.

Liquidity: Liquidity is provided on PancakeSwap V2 under the official XBTCR/WBNB pair. There are no trading restrictions, no whitelists, and no hidden mechanics.

Security: Contract is fully verified on BscScan. Ownership is under full community control. There are no blacklist functions or private minting capabilities. Liquidity may be locked or burned in future phases for transparency.

Roadmap Highlights: Token launch, website launch, liquidity setup, influencer campaigns, exchange listings (CEX/DEX), utility development, and long-term community growth with possible DAO voting.

XBTCR Whitepaper

1. Introduction

XBTCR is a decentralized, community-powered cryptocurrency built on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). Our goal is to offer a fair and transparent ecosystem without any hidden taxes, centralized control, or restrictive permissions. The XBTCR project prioritizes full decentralization and aims to be governed entirely by its holders.

XBTCR can be used for decentralized trading, community staking (future plan), governance voting in proposals, and payments in Web3 platforms. We aim to explore NFT integration and DApps in the future phases of our roadmap.

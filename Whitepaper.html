XBTCR Token – Whitepaper

1. Introduction

XBTCR is a decentralized, community-driven cryptocurrency built on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). Our mission is to deliver a transparent, trustless and tax-free token ecosystem. XBTCR empowers holders by removing unnecessary barriers such as high tax, centralized control, and hidden terms. The vision is to create a token where the community has full control, and everything is open-source and on-chain.

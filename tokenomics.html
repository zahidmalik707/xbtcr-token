Tokenomics

XBTCR was launched with a total supply of 100 trillion tokens. Out of this, 99 trillion tokens were permanently burned and removed from circulation. This leaves a final circulating supply of just 1 trillion tokens, ensuring scarcity and fairness in distribution.

The trading slippage is recommended at 2%, which allows for smooth buying and selling on PancakeSwap without failed transactions. While the base token transfers are tax-free, a total transaction tax of 3.5% is applied on DEX trades — with 2% allocated towards marketing and development, and 1.5% automatically burned to reduce supply.

Liquidity has been added to PancakeSwap V2 through the XBTCR/WBNB pair, and the contract does not contain any blacklist or whitelist functions. All trading is open and permissionless — no privileged wallets, no traps, and no limits.

Every burn is verifiable on-chain and the smart contract is publicly available on BscScan. The ownership has been renounced, ensuring that no further changes can be made to core functions — making XBTCR a fully decentralized, trustless token.

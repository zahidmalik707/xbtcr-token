About XBTCR

XBTCR is a decentralized BEP-20 token designed to bring real utility to the DeFi ecosystem. Built on Binance Smart Chain, XBTCR focuses on simplicity, transparency, and accessibility. Our mission is to create a fair and open financial tool that empowers the community. With a fixed total supply and a transparent burn mechanism, XBTCR ensures long-term value and reduced inflation. The project is community-driven with no central control, no presale, and no private investors. All tokens are launched fairly on PancakeSwap, and 99% of the total supply is permanently burned to build trust. Whether you're new to crypto or a seasoned trader, XBTCR is built for you. Buy, hold, and earn while we build tools and apps to expand the ecosystem.

What is XBTCR?

XBTCR is a decentralized, community-driven cryptocurrency on Binance Smart Chain, designed to offer freedom, fairness, and full transparency. With zero manipulation, no private wallets, and no pre-sale advantage, XBTCR gives power back to the people.

Our Mission

To create a clean and fair token ecosystem with zero restrictions, open liquidity, and community-focused decision making. We aim to lead by example with visible burns, public records, and secure contract structure.

Our Vision

We envision a world where crypto projects are no longer feared for rugs and scams. XBTCR aims to be the standard of trust — a token with no hidden code, no special permissions, and everything verifiable on-chain.

Why XBTCR is Different

  • ✅ 99% supply burned at launch
  • ✅ No team wallet, no private sale
  • ✅ Verified contract & source code
  • ✅ Liquidity added publicly
  • ✅ Public token ownership (no central control)

Who Controls XBTCR?

No one. XBTCR is owned by its holders. There are no developers holding a special key. Everything is on-chain, public, and community-governed.
